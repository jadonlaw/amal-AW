Welcome to the Amal Airways ACARS

Please read this in full before you begin flying.

Welcome aboard, and thank you for joining Amal Airways. This application is your ACARS — the Aircraft Communications Addressing and Reporting System — the same kind of tool real airlines and virtual airlines use to track flights, monitor performance, and keep operations running smoothly. Please take a few minutes to understand how it works.

What this system does

When you fly in Microsoft Flight Simulator, the ACARS reads your aircraft directly through SimConnect. It follows your entire flight from gate to gate: it verifies your pre-flight setup, tracks your position, altitude, and speed live on the crew map, watches your adherence to standard operating procedures, and grades your landing the moment your wheels touch down. When you arrive, it automatically files a flight report (a PIREP) and adds your hours to your logbook. Everything is shared through our cloud server, so your fellow pilots — whether they're across the country or across the world — can see your flight in real time.

How to use it

Start by signing in with the credentials sent to you. Head to Create Flight to set up your route — we recommend planning through SimBrief, which builds a complete, realistic flight plan and lets you prefile directly to VATSIM. Once you're set up in the sim, start the ACARS and fly. Your rank grows as you log hours, beginning as a First Officer and advancing toward Captain and beyond. Larger and heavier aircraft require higher ranks, so build your experience on smaller types first. Scheduled routes can be claimed in Routes & Hubs — claim one that matches your class, then fly it at the scheduled time.

What we expect

Fly professionally and honestly. The ACARS detects slewing, and using it will void your flight. Follow standard operating procedures — excessive violations are logged and reviewed by staff. Respect your fellow crew and the airline. Repeated violations may result in strikes.

Need help?

If you ever get stuck, have a question, or something isn't working, don't struggle alone — reach out in Discord. Ping or DM the CEO, the COO, or a Fleet Manager and someone will help you get sorted. We want you flying, not fighting with software.

By accepting below, you confirm you've read and understood how the Amal Airways ACARS works and agree to fly by our standards. Welcome to the fleet — we're glad to have you.
